About Artist

Background

Fernando Amorsolo y Cueto was born on May 30, 1892 in Paco, Manila. His mother's cousin, Don Fabián de la Rosa, was also a Filipino painter. At the young age of 13, Fernando Amorsolo became Don Fabián de la Rosa's apprentice. In time, Don Fabián de la Rosa would become the guide to further Fernando Amorsolo's career in painting. Fernando Amorsolo's first success as a painter came to be in 1908, when one of his paintings took second place at the Bazar Escolta, a contest organized by the Asociacion Internacional de Artistas.

At the age of 79, Fernando Amorsolo passed of heart failure on April 24, 1972.

To this day, he is considered as one of the most prominent artists in the history of painting in the Philippines. Fernando Amorsolo was a portraitist and painter of Philippine Landscapes. He is best known for his craftsmanship as well as mastery in the use of light.
